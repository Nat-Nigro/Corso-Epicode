Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow you taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new holiday starter.

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce


Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/3 cups lukewarn water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
